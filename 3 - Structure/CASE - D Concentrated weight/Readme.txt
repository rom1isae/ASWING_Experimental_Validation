############################################################################################################################################CASE D - Effect of concentrated masses on the modal response of a straight wing
############################################################################################################################################

This folder presents the effect of the spanwize and chordwize location of a concentrated weight on the modal response of a straight wing 
The modal analysis.xlsx presents the ASWING prediction against experimental data 

The folder Simulation_files/ contains the ASWING files if you want to reproduce the analysis.

References:
1 - The experimental data are coming from the following naca report
Runyan, H. L. and Sewall, J. L. (1948). Experi-
mental investigation of the effects of concentrated
weights on flutter characteristics of a straight can-
tilever wing. NACA Technical Note 1594, NACA,
Langley Memorial Aeronautical Laboratory, Lang-
ley Field.
