############################################################################################################################################CASE A - Helicopter blade
############################################################################################################################################

The folder is organised as follow:
    /Mesh_convergence_analysis/ present a mesh convergence analysis for both modal analysis and tip delfection
                                in order to provide you the best trade off between, efficiency and precision
                                
   /Modal_analysis/             present the comparison of the modal analysis of ASWING in comparison with the
                                experimental data. 
 
  /Static_dedlfection/          present the comparison of the static deflection and twist prediction of ASWING in 
                                comparison with the experimental data.

References : The experimental data and beams properties are extracted from the following references

1 - Minguet, P. J. (1989). Static and Dynamic Be-
havior of Composite Helicopter Rotor Blades under
Large Deflections. PhD thesis, MIT, Boston, MA.

2 -Minguet, P. and Dugundji, J. (1990a). Exper-
iments and analysis for composite blades under
large deflections. I - Static behavior. AIAA Jour-
nal,

3 - Minguet, P. and Dugundji, J. (1990b). Experi-
ments and analysis for composite blades under large
deflections. II - Dynamic behavior. AIAA Journal,
